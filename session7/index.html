5 Inspiring Web Artists

Orion Martin, Bakers Steak, 2015

The painting depicts a combination of objects and flowers creating an interesting composition.

Gertrude Abercrombie, Out in the Open, 1939

The surreal elements in this piece create a dreamlike atmosphere.

Peter Young, Capitalist Masterpiece N 19, 1969

The dotted technique used in the painting creates movement and texture

Ashley Zelinskie, Twin Quasar, 2024

The combination of images creates a sense of movement and visual flow.

Jack Youngerman, Blue April Yellow, 1970

This painting has a strong contrast between the blue and yellow, and the organic shapes create a sense of balance.

Bio

Domenica Vazquez is a student of Emerging Media at Hunter College. Previously, she studied Fine Arts with a concentration in Sculpture, where she developed projects that fueled her creativity and deepened her interest in material processes. In her free time, she enjoys hobbies like knitting and sewing, as well as spending time with her two cats, who are an integral part of her everyday life.
